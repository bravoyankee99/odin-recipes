Vietnamese Fresh Spring Rolls

Description

This chicken taco soup is similar to chili and tastes wonderful! This recipe is also very adaptable to your personal taste.

Ingredient

  • 2 (10 ounce) cans diced tomatoes with green chiles, undrained
  • 1 (16 ounce) can chili beans
  • 1 (15 ounce) can black beans

Steps

  1. lace diced tomatoes, chili beans, black beans, corn, beer, tomato sauce, and onion into a slow cooker. Add taco seasoning; stir to blend. Lay chicken breasts on top, pressing down slightly until just covered.
  2. Cover and cook on Low for 5 hours.
